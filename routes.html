Must-Visit Destinations

Canada offers diverse landscapes, from the breathtaking Rocky Mountains to the serene coastlines of Vancouver Island. Nature lovers can explore Banff, Jasper, and the iconic Niagara Falls for unforgettable outdoor adventures.

Vibrant cities like Toronto and Montreal blend rich culture with modern attractions. Experience Quebec City's historic charm, or enjoy the lively arts scene and multicultural vibe of Canada’s urban centers.

Whether hiking in the Yukon or enjoying coastal beauty in Prince Edward Island, Canada has something for every traveler. With diverse landscapes and rich culture, each destination offers unique, unforgettable experiences.

Discover Canada's Beauty

Experience the breathtaking landscapes and vibrant cities.

  • Canadian Rockies Adventure

    Adventure  —  Nature   —  Hiking

    4 DAYS — 300 km

    Vancouver > Banff > Jasper

    Embark on a breathtaking journey through the Canadian Rockies. Enjoy scenic drives, hiking in national parks, and exploring picturesque lakes and wildlife.

  • DAY 1

    Explore Vancouver

    Start your adventure in the vibrant city of Vancouver. Explore the bustling downtown area, visit Stanley Park, and enjoy the stunning views from the Vancouver Lookout.

    Discover the diverse neighborhoods, including Gastown and Chinatown, and indulge in the local cuisine at one of the many restaurants and cafés.

    For those looking for more adventure, take a bike ride along the seawall or go kayaking in the nearby waters. Don't forget to visit the Capilano Suspension Bridge for a thrilling experience.

  • DAY 2

    Explore Banff

    Begin your day in the charming town of Banff. Visit the Banff National Park and take a scenic drive along the Icefields Parkway.

    Explore the stunning Lake Louise and Moraine Lake, known for their turquoise waters and breathtaking mountain views.

    For those seeking adventure, hike the trails around Johnston Canyon or take a gondola ride up Sulphur Mountain for panoramic views of the Rockies.

  • DAY 3

    Explore Jasper

    Continue your adventure in the picturesque town of Jasper. Visit the Jasper National Park and take a scenic drive along the Icefields Parkway.

    Explore the stunning Maligne Lake and Spirit Island, known for their crystal-clear waters and breathtaking mountain views.

    For those seeking adventure, hike the trails around Maligne Canyon or take a boat tour on Maligne Lake for a unique perspective of the Rockies.

  • Eastern Canada Discovery

    Culture  —  History   —  Nature

    5 DAYS — 400 km

    Toronto > Ottawa > Montreal > Quebec

    Discover the rich history and vibrant culture of Eastern Canada. From the bustling streets of Toronto to the charming Old Quebec, this journey takes you through iconic landmarks, scenic landscapes, and cultural experiences that define the region.

  • DAY 1

    Discover Toronto

    Explore the vibrant city of Toronto. Visit the iconic CN Tower for breathtaking views. Stroll through the historic Distillery District and lively Nathan Phillips Square.

    Experience the city's cultural diversity in Kensington Market and Chinatown. Don’t miss a trip to the stunning Niagara Falls, just an hour away.

    For adventure seekers, take a boat ride to the base of the falls or cross the thrilling Whirlpool Aero Car.

  • DAY 2

    Explore Ottawa

    Arrive in Canada’s capital, Ottawa. Visit the majestic Parliament Hill and take a walk along the Rideau Canal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

    Discover Canadian heritage at the Canadian Museum of History and learn about Indigenous culture.

    For nature lovers, hike the scenic trails of Gatineau Park or go kayaking on the Ottawa River.

  • DAY 3

    Explore Montreal

    Immerse yourself in the cultural hub of Montreal. Wander through the charming Old Montreal and admire the stunning Notre-Dame Basilica.

    Explore the modern Place des Arts and indulge in local flavors at Jean-Talon Market.

    For a panoramic city view, hike up Mount Royal, a must-see spot for breathtaking scenery.

  • DAY 4

    Explore Quebec City

    Travel to the picturesque city of Quebec. Stroll through the historic Old Quebec, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

    Walk along the Dufferin Terrace, offering incredible views of the Château Frontenac.

    Experience the natural beauty of Montmorency Falls, one of Canada’s highest waterfalls.

  • Atlantic Coastal Adventure

    Ocean   —  Wildlife   —  Scenic Views

    4 DAYS — 400 km

    Halifax > Peggy’s Cove > Cape Breton > Prince Edward Island

    Experience the rugged beauty of Canada’s Atlantic coast. From historic port cities to breathtaking coastal views, this adventure offers a perfect blend of maritime culture, natural wonders, and unforgettable oceanfront landscapes.

  • DAY 1

    Discover Halifax

    Start your journey in Halifax, the vibrant capital of Nova Scotia. Visit the historic Citadel Hill and explore the Maritime Museum of the Atlantic.

    Take a relaxing walk along the Halifax Waterfront Boardwalk and enjoy fresh seafood at the local restaurants.

    For breathtaking ocean views, take a ferry ride to Dartmouth and enjoy the coastal scenery.

  • DAY 2

    Visit Peggy’s Cove

    Travel to the iconic Peggy’s Cove, home to one of the most famous lighthouses in Canada. Explore the rugged coastline and take stunning photos of the Atlantic waves crashing against the rocks.

    Enjoy fresh lobster at a local seafood shack while taking in the salty ocean breeze.

    For adventure lovers, go kayaking along the shoreline or explore the charming fishing villages nearby.

  • DAY 3

    Explore Cape Breton Island

    Drive along the breathtaking Cabot Trail, one of the most scenic routes in North America. Stop at Cape Breton Highlands National Park for hiking and wildlife spotting.

    Visit the charming town of Baddeck, home to the Alexander Graham Bell Museum.

    Enjoy a traditional Ceilidh (Celtic music gathering) for an authentic taste of Nova Scotia's culture.
